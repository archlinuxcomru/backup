own_window_transparent no
own_window_colour 2D2D2D

Короче как-то так:

use_xft yes 
xftfont Sans:size=9 
 
own_window yes
own_window_transparent no
own_window_type desktop 
own_window_hints undecorated,below,sticky,skip_taskbar,skip_pager 
double_buffer yes 
color1 ff0000
color2 00ff00
color3 0000ff
own_window_colour 000000
############################################## 
 
TEXT
${color1}red${color}  ${color2}green${color} ${color3}blue${color} ${alignr}${color #ff0000}red${color}  ${color #00ff00}green${color} ${color #0000ff}blue${color}
