поставить synaptics.
править хоргконф.

Section “InputDevice”
Identifier “synaptics0”
Driver “synaptics”
Option “SendCoreEvents” “true”
Option “Device” “/dev/psaux”
Option “Protocol” “auto-dev”
Option “HorizEdgeScroll” “1”
Option “SHMConfig” “true”
EndSection

в Section “ServerLayout” добавить InputDevice “synaptics0”

и поставь gsynaptics.
