Либо попробуй
systemd-analyze critical-chain
The time when unit became active or started is printed after the "@" character.
The time the unit took to start is printed after the "+" character.

graphical.target @3.239s
└─multi-user.target @3.239s
  └─systemd-swap.service @1.545s +1.693s
    └─basic.target @1.539s
      └─sockets.target @1.539s
        └─dbus.socket @1.539s
          └─sysinit.target @1.538s
            └─sys-fs-fuse-connections.mount @15.001s +3ms
              └─systemd-modules-load.service @371ms +88ms
                └─systemd-journald.socket
                  └─-.mount
                    └─system.slice
                      └─-.slice
Показывает ветку сервисов которая больше всего тормозит загрузку.
