[email protected]:~$ bluetoothctl
Agent registered
[bluetooth]# power on
[CHG] Controller 00:1A:7D:DA:71:0A Class: 0x000c0104
Changing power on succeeded
[CHG] Controller 00:1A:7D:DA:71:0A Powered: yes
[bluetooth]# devices
Device 5E:4E:46:03:26:BD S8
[bluetooth]# pair 5E:4E:46:03:26:BD
Attempting to pair with 5E:4E:46:03:26:BD
Failed to pair: org.bluez.Error.AlreadyExists
[bluetooth]# trust 5E:4E:46:03:26:BD
Changing 5E:4E:46:03:26:BD trust succeeded
[bluetooth]# connect 5E:4E:46:03:26:BD
Attempting to connect to 5E:4E:46:03:26:BD
[CHG] Device 5E:4E:46:03:26:BD Connected: yes
Connection successful
[CHG] Device 5E:4E:46:03:26:BD ServicesResolved: yes
[S8]#
