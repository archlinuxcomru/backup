Бесполезно, причина в другом .... нагуглил похожий случай ...
Насколько понял, причина в самой видеокарте ... выпендривается ... вот что пишет сам разработчик hwinfo
Over the time, there were many bug reports that mode detection interferes with the regular graphics card operation (the X server). So in the end I decided to turn off gfx card memory and port access in the emulation code so no harm can come to your graphical desktop.
For most of the cards this is sufficient to read the mode list as they basically just return a pointer to some memory area. But some want to have a look at their internal state before they hand out data. This of course fails as the emulation does not access the real card at all. And you get no or incomplete data, as in your case.
I cannot re-enable real hardware access unconditionally due to this. But adding some option to hwinfo would be ok.
И если перейти в tty, то должно все получится .... о чем предположил еще выше и просил проверить ..
