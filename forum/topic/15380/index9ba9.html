+ вот это тоже вроде:
https://www.archlinux.org/packages/extra/x86_64/dnsmasq/

ebtables and dnsmasq for the default NAT/DHCP networking.
bridge-utils for bridged networking.
openbsd-netcat for remote management over SSH.
